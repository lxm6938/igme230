University News

ROLE:

Visual Designer

YEAR:

Fall 2017

The RIT University News site is boring, poorly designed, and overloaded with content. For this project, I was tasked with visualy redesiging the homepage of RIT University News.

This project was a great exercise on hierarchal and gridded content, and required me to think in terms of what a visitor to RIT University News would most want to see. I used Adobe Illustrator to achieve the final result over a three week period.
