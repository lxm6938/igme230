ROLE:

UX/UI Designer

YEAR:

Spring 2018

In response to the Hawaii missile alert that shocked the nation last year, I was tasked with designing an emergency alert system solution that could mitigate the potential for a mistake of that magnitude to occur again.

This project required extensive analysis of what exactly happened during the Hawaii missile crisis, and was a good challenge in putting myself into the minds of my users. I used Adobe illustrator to achieve my final result across a three week period.
