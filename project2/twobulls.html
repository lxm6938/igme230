Two Bulls

ROLE:

Creative Intern

YEAR:

Summer 2018

During the Summer of 2018, I had the privilege of being a Creative Intern at Two Bulls. Located in Dumbo, Two Bulls is an agency that focuses on merging design with engineering to build products that far exceed expectations.

As a creative intern, I worked on redesigning the approach of how Two Bulls employees use company templates from a UX research perspective. This required me to conduct intense research on the users and their templates, user interviews of employees, and many design exercises to help create a solution.
